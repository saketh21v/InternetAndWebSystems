What is a neutron star?

Asked by Sujit Semakurthi

A neutron star is a very small and dense star made almost completely of nuetrons. They are small stars with radius about 11–11.5 km. They have a mass of about twice if that of the sun. They are smallest and densest stars known to exist in the universe. They are what is left after a huge star which exploded as a supernova.
